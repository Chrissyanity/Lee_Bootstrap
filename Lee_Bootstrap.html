True Life: I'm addicted to Sherlock.

What's better than American television shows? BBC television shows.

Although I enjoy most of the shows the BBC has to offer, Sherlock is by far my FAVORITE. As you may have guessed from the title, Sherlock is a modern rendition of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's Sherlock Holmes detective stories. The show stars Benedict Cumberbatch as Sherlock Holmes and Martin Freeman as Sherlock's famous sidekick, Dr. John Watson. Each season only consists of three episodes and although the series began in 2010, we have only just started season four, which may be the last one.

If you're anything like me and love putting your deduction skills to the test no matter how elementary they may be, you likely will be addicted to Sherlock just as much as me.
